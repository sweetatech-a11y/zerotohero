Google Business Profile — Setup Guide

How to claim your business on Google & grant agency access
Your Logo Here

Your Google Business Profile (formerly Google My Business) is how your business appears on Google Search and Google Maps. This guide walks you through claiming or creating your profile and giving us access to manage it. The process takes about 10–15 minutes (verification may take a few extra days).

📋 What You'll Need Before Starting

  • A Google account (Gmail or Google Workspace email)
  • Your exact business name as it appears to customers
  • Your business address (or service area if you travel to customers)
  • Your business phone number and website URL
  • Your business hours
  • A few photos of your business (storefront, team, work examples)
Part 1 — Claim or Create Your Google Business Profile
1

Go to Google Business Profile Manager

Open your browser and go to https://business.google.com

Sign in with your Google account.

2

Search for your business

Type in your business name. If your business already appears in Google's directory, click on it to claim it. If it doesn't appear, click "Add your business to Google".

3

Enter your business details

Fill in your business name (exactly as customers know it), select your business category (e.g., "Plumber", "Roofing Contractor", "Restaurant").

Choose the most specific category available — you can add more categories later.

4

Add your location

If customers visit your business: Enter your full street address.

If you go to customers (service area business): Skip the street address and instead define your service areas by city, zip code, or region.

5

Add your contact information

Enter your business phone number and website URL. These will appear on your Google listing.

6

Verify your business

Google requires you to verify ownership. Common methods include:

Phone/Text: Google calls or texts a code to your business number (fastest).

Email: Google sends a code to your business email.

Postcard: Google mails a postcard with a code to your address (takes 5–14 days).

Video: Record a short video of your business location and signage.

⚠ Your profile won't appear on Google until verification is complete. Choose the fastest method available to you.

7

Complete your profile

After verification, add your business hours, a business description (750 characters max), photos, and services. The more complete your profile, the better you'll rank in local search results.

Already have a Google Business Profile? If you've already verified your business on Google, skip Part 1 and go directly to Part 2 to grant us access.

Part 2 — Grant Us Access to Your Profile
1

Go to your Google Business Profile

Navigate to https://business.google.com and sign in. Select your business if you have multiple locations.

2

Open Business Profile settings

Click on "Business Profile settings" in the menu, then click "People and access" (or "Managers").

3

Click the "Add" button

Click the "+" or "Add" button to invite a new user.

4

Enter our email address and set role to "Manager"

Enter the email address we provide (shown in the green box below). Set the role to "Manager".

Manager allows us to update your profile info, respond to reviews, add photos, and create posts — everything needed to optimize your presence. You remain the Owner and can remove our access at any time.

5

Click "Invite"

Click "Invite". We'll receive an email invitation and accept it on our end.
