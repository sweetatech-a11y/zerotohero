TikTok Ads — Setup Guide

How to create your TikTok Ads account & grant agency access
Your Logo Here

This guide walks you through creating a TikTok Business Center and Ads Manager account, then giving us the access we need to manage your TikTok ad campaigns. The process takes about 10–15 minutes.

📋 What You'll Need Before Starting

  • An email address for your business account
  • Your business name, website URL, and industry
  • A credit or debit card for billing
  • Your business phone number
Part 1 — Create Your TikTok Ads Manager Account
1

Go to TikTok Ads Manager

Open your browser and go to https://ads.tiktok.com

Click "Create now" or "Get started".

2

Sign up with your business email

Enter your business email address and create a password. You can also sign up with your phone number. Verify your email by entering the code sent to your inbox.

3

Fill in your business information

Enter your business/company name, select your industry, add your business website URL, and enter your phone number.

Select your country/region, time zone, and currency.

⚠ Time zone and currency cannot be changed after setup — double-check before confirming.

4

Accept terms and submit

Review TikTok's advertising terms, check the agreement box, and click "Register" or "Submit".

5

Set up billing

Once your account is created, go to "Payment" in the top menu and add your credit or debit card. You may need to choose between Automatic or Manual payment — select Automatic for most businesses.

6

Note your Advertiser Account ID

In the top-right corner of TikTok Ads Manager, you'll see your Advertiser Account ID (a number). Write this down — we'll need it.

Part 2 — Create a TikTok Business Center (if not already created)
1

Go to TikTok Business Center

Navigate to https://business.tiktok.com

Click "Create Business Center" if you don't have one yet.

2

Enter your Business Center name

Use your company name. Select your time zone and click "Create".

3

Add your Ad Account to the Business Center

In Business Center, go to "Assets" → "Ad accounts" → "Add ad account". Choose "Add from Ads Manager" and select the account you just created.

Part 3 — Grant Us Partner Access
1

Go to your Business Center settings

In TikTok Business Center, click on "Members" in the left sidebar, then go to the "Partners" tab.

2

Click "Add partner"

Click the "Add partner" button. Select "Invite a partner to access your assets".

3

Enter our Business Center ID

Enter our TikTok Business Center ID (shown in the green box below). Click "Next".

4

Assign ad account access

Select your Ad Account and grant "Ad Account Operator" access. This allows us to create, manage, and optimize your ad campaigns.

5

Confirm and send the invitation

Review the permissions and click "Send Invitation". We'll accept the invite on our end.
