Google Ads — Setup Guide

How to create your Google Ads account & grant agency access
Your Logo Here

This guide walks you through creating a Google Ads account and giving us the access we need to manage your advertising campaigns. The whole process takes about 10–15 minutes.

📋 What You'll Need Before Starting

  • A Google account (Gmail or Google Workspace email)
  • Your business name and website URL
  • A credit or debit card for billing
  • Your business address and phone number
Part 1 — Create Your Google Ads Account
1

Go to Google Ads

Open your browser and navigate to https://ads.google.com

Click "Start now" or "Sign in" if you already have a Google account.

2

Sign in with your Google account

Use a Google account that belongs to your business (not a personal Gmail if possible). If you don't have one, click "Create account" to make a new one.

⚠ Use an email you'll have permanent access to — this becomes the owner of the ad account.

3

Skip the guided campaign setup

Google will try to walk you through creating your first campaign. Look for the link that says "Switch to Expert Mode" at the bottom of the page and click it.

Then click "Create an account without a campaign" — we will build your campaigns for you.

4

Confirm your business information

Select your country, time zone, and currency. Make sure the time zone matches your business location.

⚠ These settings cannot be changed later, so double-check before confirming.

5

Set up billing

Enter your credit or debit card information. This is the card that will be charged for your ad spend.

Fill in your billing address and business details as prompted. Click "Submit" when done.

6

Your account is created!

You'll see your Google Ads dashboard. Take note of your Customer ID — it's the 10-digit number (XXX-XXX-XXXX) in the top-right corner of the screen. We'll need this number.

Part 2 — Grant Us Access to Your Account
1

Go to Account Access settings

In your Google Ads dashboard, click the Admin icon (wrench/gear) in the left sidebar, then click "Access and security".

2

Click the blue "+" button to invite a user

Click the blue "+" button, then select "Invite new user" or "Grant access to a manager account".

3

Enter our agency email address

Type in the email address we provide to you (shown in the green box below).

4

Set the access level to "Admin"

Select "Admin" from the access level dropdown. This allows us to create and manage campaigns, adjust budgets, and optimize your ads.

5

Send the invitation

Click "Send invitation". We'll receive an email and accept the invite on our end.
