Meta Ads — Setup Guide

How to set up Facebook Business Manager & grant agency access
Your Logo Here

This guide walks you through setting up Meta Business Manager (which manages both Facebook and Instagram advertising) and giving us the access we need to run your ad campaigns. The process takes about 15–20 minutes.

📋 What You'll Need Before Starting

  • A personal Facebook account (required to create a Business Manager — it stays private)
  • Your business name, address, and website URL
  • A credit or debit card for ad billing
  • Access to your Facebook Business Page (if you have one already)
  • Access to your Instagram Business account (if applicable)
Part 1 — Create Your Meta Business Manager
1

Go to Meta Business Suite

Open your browser and go to https://business.facebook.com

Click "Create an account" (or log in if you already have a Business Manager).

2

Log in with your personal Facebook account

Meta requires a personal Facebook account to verify your identity. Your personal profile is never visible to us or used in ads — it simply proves you're a real person.

3

Enter your business details

Fill in your Business name, your name, and business email address. Click "Submit".

Check your email inbox and confirm the verification email from Meta.

4

Add your Facebook Page to Business Manager

Go to Settings (gear icon) → "Accounts" → "Pages" → "Add".

If you own the page: choose "Add a Page" and select it. If someone else manages it, choose "Request Access to a Page".

5

Connect your Instagram account (if applicable)

Go to Settings → "Accounts" → "Instagram accounts" → "Add".

Log in with your Instagram credentials to link it to your Business Manager.

6

Create an Ad Account (if you don't have one)

Go to Settings → "Accounts" → "Ad accounts" → "Add" → "Create a new ad account".

Name it your business name, select your time zone and currency.

⚠ Time zone and currency cannot be changed after creation — double-check before confirming.

7

Add a payment method

Inside your Ad Account, go to "Payment settings" and add your credit or debit card. This card will be charged for your ad spend.

Part 2 — Grant Us Partner Access

Important: Partner access is the proper way to grant an agency access. Never share your personal login credentials — partner access keeps your account secure and lets you revoke access at any time.

1

Go to Business Settings

In Business Manager, click the gear icon (Settings) in the bottom-left corner.

2

Navigate to Partners

In the left menu under "Users", click "Partners".

3

Click "Add" and enter our Business ID

Click the "Add" button, then select "Give a partner access to your assets".

Enter our Meta Business ID (shown in the green box below).

4

Assign access to your Ad Account

After entering our Business ID, you'll see an asset assignment screen. Select "Ad accounts", find your ad account, and toggle on "Manage campaigns" access.

5

Assign access to your Facebook Page

While still in the partner assignment screen, click "Pages", select your business page, and toggle on "Manage Page" and "Create content".

6

Assign access to your Instagram account

Click "Instagram accounts", select your account, and toggle on "Manage Instagram account".

7

Assign access to your Pixel (if it exists)

Click "Pixels", select your Meta Pixel, and toggle on "Manage Pixel". If you don't have a pixel yet, that's fine — we'll create one.

8

Click "Save Changes"

Review all assignments and click "Save Changes". We'll now be able to see and manage the assets you've shared.
