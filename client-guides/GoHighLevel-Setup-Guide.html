GoHighLevel — Client Setup Guide

How to activate your CRM account & complete onboarding

We use GoHighLevel (GHL) as your all-in-one CRM and lead management platform. It handles your lead tracking, automated follow-ups, appointment booking, reviews, and more. This guide walks you through activating your account and completing the initial setup. The process takes about 15–20 minutes.

How this works: We create and manage your GHL sub-account from our agency dashboard. You'll receive a login invitation from us — you don't need to sign up for GHL separately. We handle the technical setup; you just need to verify your info and complete a few steps.

📋 What You'll Need Before Starting

  • The login email we sent you (check your inbox for the invitation)
  • Your business name, address, and phone number
  • Your business logo (high-quality PNG or JPG)
  • A credit or debit card (for phone/SMS costs if applicable)
  • Your business hours
  • Your preferred calendar availability for appointments
Part 1 — Activate Your GHL Account
1

Check your email for the invitation

We'll send you an email invitation from GoHighLevel with the subject line containing your login details. Click the link in the email to set your password.

If you don't see it, check your spam/junk folder.

2

Set your password and log in

3

Explore your dashboard

Once logged in, you'll see your CRM dashboard. This is where all your leads, conversations, appointments, and reports live. Take a moment to look around — we'll walk you through everything during onboarding.

Part 2 — Complete Your Business Profile
1

Verify your business information

Go to Settings (gear icon) → "Business Profile". Verify that your business name, address, phone number, and website URL are correct. Update anything that's missing or incorrect.

2

Upload your business logo

In Business Profile settings, upload your logo. This will appear on emails, landing pages, invoices, and booking pages sent to your customers.

3

Set your business hours

In Business Profile, set your operating hours. This affects when automated messages are sent and when customers can book appointments.

Part 3 — Connect Your Phone Number (for calls & texts)
1

Register for A2P 10DLC (required for SMS in the US)

Go to Settings → "Phone Numbers". You'll need to complete a business registration for A2P 10DLC compliance. This is a one-time regulatory requirement for sending text messages.

You'll need your business EIN (Tax ID), legal business name, and business address.

⚠ Registration can take 1–7 business days for approval. We recommend doing this first.

2

Choose your phone number option

Option A: We can set up a new local phone number inside GHL for you (recommended — all calls and texts are tracked).

Option B: If you want to keep your existing business number, we can port it into GHL or set up call forwarding.

Let us know your preference and we'll handle the setup.

Part 4 — Set Up Your Calendar
1

Connect your Google or Outlook calendar

Go to Settings → "Integrations" → "Google" (or "Outlook"). Click "Connect" and sign in with the calendar account you use for scheduling.

This syncs your availability so customers never book when you're busy.

2

Set your availability

Go to Calendars in the main menu. We'll have a booking calendar pre-configured for you. Review the available time slots and adjust to match when you're available for appointments.

Part 5 — Download the Mobile App
1

Install the GHL mobile app

Search for "GoHighLevel" (or your agency's branded app name) in the App Store (iPhone) or Google Play Store (Android). Download and install it.

2

Log in with your credentials

Use the same email and password from Part 1. The mobile app lets you see new leads, respond to messages, and manage appointments from your phone.

3

Enable push notifications

When prompted, allow notifications. This ensures you get instant alerts when new leads come in or customers send messages. Speed-to-lead is critical — responding within 5 minutes dramatically increases your close rate.
