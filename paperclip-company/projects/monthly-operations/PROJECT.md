# Monthly Agency Operations Project

This is the agency's recurring monthly operating cycle. It runs from the 25th of each month through the 7th of the following month. Every active client receives a full monthly review, a performance report, and a marketing sprint plan for the next month.

## Monthly Cycle Timeline

### Week 4 of the Month (25th–31st): Data Collection & Draft Reports

| Date | Agent | Task |
|------|-------|------|
| 25th | Brand Monitor Specialist | Deliver share-of-voice report to Reporting Director |
| 26th | All Specialists | Compile final month data (final rank check, final ad data, final pipeline pull) |
| 27th | Reporting Director | Begin pulling all API data for monthly reports |
| 28th | Reporting Director | Draft all client reports |
| 29th–30th | All Directors | Submit any context notes to Reporting Director (campaign changes, notable events) |

### Month Start (1st–3rd): Report Approval & Delivery

| Date | Agent | Task |
|------|-------|------|
| 1st | Reporting Director | Deliver all draft reports to COO approval queue |
| 2nd | COO | Reviews reports, queues for board approval |
| 3rd | Board | Reviews and approves reports |
| 3rd–4th | Reporting Director | Triggers GHL email automation to deliver reports to clients |

### Week 1 of New Month (1st–7th): Sprint Planning & Strategy

| Date | Agent | Task |
|------|-------|------|
| 1st | COO | Reviews previous month performance across all clients, sets company-level priorities |
| 2nd | Marketing Director | Produces monthly marketing sprint plan per client |
| 2nd | CRM Director | Reviews pipeline health, queues any workflow improvements |
| 2nd | Reputation Director | Reviews reputation scores, updates recovery plans if needed |
| 3rd–4th | COO | Weekly digest to board: last month summary + this month priorities |
| 5th–7th | All Specialists | Begin executing the new month's sprint plans |

## Monthly Review Checklist (per client)

The COO uses this checklist to evaluate each client's month:

**Marketing:**
- [ ] Are rankings for priority keywords improving?
- [ ] Is GBP post cadence on track (4 posts/month)?
- [ ] Are paid ads within budget and hitting CPL target?
- [ ] Are there pending creative briefs that need board review?

**CRM:**
- [ ] Is speed-to-contact above 70%?
- [ ] Is the lead-to-appointment rate above 25%?
- [ ] Are there stale contacts in the pipeline (>14 days no activity)?
- [ ] Are there any failed automations to address?

**Reputation:**
- [ ] Is the Google rating at or above 4.0?
- [ ] Are all reviews getting responses within 48 hours of being drafted?
- [ ] Are any negative mentions requiring escalation?
- [ ] Is review request volume sufficient to maintain or grow the rating?

**Reporting:**
- [ ] Was the monthly report delivered on time (by the 4th)?
- [ ] Did the report translate all metrics into plain English?
- [ ] Is the client's next-month goal section accurate and realistic?

## Success Criteria

- All client reports delivered to clients by the 5th of the month
- Sprint plans finalized for all clients by the 7th
- Board approval queue cleared (no items pending >48 hours) by end of week 1
- All director weekly summaries delivered to COO by Monday of each week
